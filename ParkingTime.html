Parking made simple with digital P-disk

P-disk is a system of allowing time-restricted free parking showing the time at which the vehicle was parked.

INTRODUCTION


Parking Time Sweden is a company that made a modernized take on the traditional P-disk. Our task was to enhance the app's functionality and its design, all while keeping the company's primary goal in mind:

Attracting more users to transition from the traditional paper P-disk to the digital alternative!

By prioritizing simplicity and ease of use, we've developed a digital P-disk that not only simplifies the parking process but also encourages users to embrace the digital transition with confidence and convenience. One less thing to think about!

CURRENT CHALLENGES

CHALLENGE #1


Limited visibility and appeal

Drivers are used to a traditional paper P-disk. Digital P-disk by Parking time Sweden is not very well known to the wider public. Only around 1000+ downloads on Play store. This directly impacts the conversion rate.

Brand Recognition
