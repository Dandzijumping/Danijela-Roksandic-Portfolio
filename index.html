About me


My name is Danijela, UX/UI designer currently studying Experience Design at Changemaker Educations in Stockholm, Sweden.

Originally from Belgrade, Serbia I moved to Stockholm in the autumn 2016 and have been working as an architect until beginning of 2023 when i decided to change my career. I have always liked to make stuff. I like to keep myself busy and on the go. Write poems and stories, design clothes, make something new out of the old.
